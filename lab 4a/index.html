Pet Care Companion

Expert tips for your furry and feathered friends

Canine Care

Daily Exercise

Dogs need at least 30 minutes to 2 hours of exercise daily, depending on the breed.

Nutrition

Feed high-quality dog food appropriate for their age, size, and activity level.

Grooming

Regular brushing and nail trimming are essential for your dog's health and comfort.

Feline Friends

Enrichment

Provide scratching posts and interactive toys to keep your cat mentally stimulated.

Litter Box

Keep the litter box clean. The general rule is one box per cat plus one extra.

Hydration

Cats often prefer running water. Consider a water fountain to encourage drinking.

Avian Care

Cage Size

Ensure the cage is large enough for your bird to fully extend its wings and fly a bit.

Diet Variety

Seeds aren't enough! Offer pellets, fresh vegetables, and some fruits.

Socialization

Birds are social creatures. Spend time talking to and playing with them daily.
